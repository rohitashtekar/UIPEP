What is ICANN fee?

The Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers ICANN charges a mandatory annual fee of $0.18 for each year of domain registration, renewal or transfer. Where applicable, the ICANN fee will be added to the listed price for the domain at the time of purchase and itemized in your shopping cart. A full list of affected domains is provided below.
